# Business Analysis

## Purpose

Provide a reusable business analysis framework that connects requirements, glossary, workflows, contracts, tests, and decisions.

## Context

Use this file to plan business analysis work before implementation. It should prevent agents from inventing business rules when the domain is still unclear.

## Artifact Index

| Artifact | Purpose | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Business glossary | Shared business language | draft |
| BPMN | Business process flow | draft |

## Analysis Questions

| Area | Question | Target Artifact |
|---|---|---|
| Problem | What pain, goal, or opportunity is being addressed? | Requirement |
| Actor | Who performs or receives value from the workflow? | Use case, glossary |
| Flow | What steps, decisions, and exception paths exist? | BPMN |
| Rule | What business rule changes behavior or validation? | Requirement, acceptance criteria |
| Data | What input, output, ownership, and sensitivity matter? | Data governance, contract |
| Evidence | How will the behavior be verified or accepted? | QA matrix, tests, DoD |

## Promotion Rules

Promote business analysis when it becomes accepted behavior, terminology, workflow, or acceptance criteria. Keep exploratory notes in task workspace until reviewed.
